Pizhichil

Treatment Details

Known as the "oil bath," Pizhichil is one of the most luxurious and rejuvenating Ayurvedic treatments. Warm herbal oil is continuously poured over the entire body while being gently massaged into the skin. This royal treatment was traditionally reserved for the kings of Kerala for its remarkable rejuvenating effects.

This treatment is especially beneficial for:

  • Arthritis and joint pain
  • Nervous system disorders
  • Muscle tension and stiffness
  • Paralysis and hemiplegia
  • Enhancing skin luster and texture
  • Overall rejuvenation and anti-aging

Procedure

The treatment begins with a brief massage to prepare the body. The patient then lies on a wooden treatment table while 2-4 therapists simultaneously pour warm medicated oil from small pieces of cloth soaked in the oil. This continuous stream of oil promotes deep relaxation, nourishes tissues, and allows therapeutic substances in the oil to penetrate deep into the skin. A typical session lasts 45-60 minutes and is usually recommended for 7-21 days for optimal results.

Treatment Details

In this traditional Ayurvedic treatment, Njavara rice (a medicinal variety of rice) is cooked in milk decoction with herbs, then placed in muslin cloth bags. These boluses are dipped in warm herbal oil or milk and applied all over the body with a special rhythmic massage technique.

The treatment lasts about 45-60 minutes and is highly effective for:

  • Neurological disorders like paralysis
  • Muscle wasting and weakness
  • Joint pain and inflammation
  • Improving blood circulation
  • Rejuvenating the body tissues
  • Strengthening the nervous system

Procedure

Before treatment, the Njavara rice is prepared through a special cooking process with milk and herbs according to traditional methods. The patient lies on a wooden massage table while two therapists simultaneously massage the body using the prepared rice boluses. The treatment usually requires a course of 7-14 days for optimal benefits.
