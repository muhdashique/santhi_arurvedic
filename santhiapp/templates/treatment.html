Panchakarma

Panchakarma is the practice of five-fold Treatment. This treatment systematically eliminates toxins from the body. These toxins (or ama) are said to cause weakness, and purify the body and keep the system aglow the natural rhythm of life. Our experts plan each detail of panchakarma treatment to suit to the whole body. It needs proper supervision and planning of systematic cleaning of toxins that have accumulated on Panchakarma.

During panchakarma, we advise on the ideal program for each patient who we analyze systematically through pulse diagnosis (Nadi pareeksha), a thorough consultation including personal/medical history comprising of therapeutic massage, dietary counseling and body manipulation as rejuvenating treatment. The average duration lasts between 7 and 21 days.

Our experienced workmen or treatment givers all treatments under the supervision of professional Ayurvedic physicians.

Swedanam-medicated Steam Bath

Swedanam, a traditional ayurvedic treatment, involves a medicated steam bath to detoxify the body. Different combinations of herbs are boiled in water, producing steam that opens pores and enhances blood circulation. The herbs used depend on the patient's ailments, improving oxygenation, sweat gland function, and toxins from the body. Swedanam also improves circulation, removes toxins, and relieves muscle stiffness. It's particularly beneficial for respiratory conditions, colds, aches, stiffness, flu, detoxifying and rejuvenating the body. Swedanam supports holistic, well-being and comfort. Benefits include relaxation, muscle pain relief, and improved circulation.

Abhyangam

Abhyangam is an ancient ayurvedic treatment that involves a unique full-body oil massage to restore energy balance and rejuvenate the body. We select warm, herbal oils based on individual needs. Our trained therapeutic massage techniques are designed for maximum effectiveness. Abhyangam aims to increase circulation and energy flow throughout the body. It helps manage energy flow, promoting relaxation and rejuvenation. Supportive pressure techniques are used to enhance physical and mental health. Regular sessions are recommended for optimal benefits.
